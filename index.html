Welcome to the introduction of Sel!

Sel is an original character I've began writing in 2024. At first, I've been drawing her out of sheer boredom in class, with those drawings being really basic and inconsistent. A bit later, I've found myself in the Hyun's Dojo community, which inspired me to register her as a duelist, although doing that needs lore and demos. This is why I created this website. To easily collect everything there is to know about Sel. Keep in mind, that this project is still work in progress though!
